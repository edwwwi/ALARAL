# ALARAL

**ALARAL** is a beautifully crafted all-in-one productivity and wellness application built with Flutter. Designed with a premium black-and-white aesthetic, ALARAL combines a powerful alarm system, intelligent reminders, sleep insights, and water tracking into a single minimal experience.

Unlike traditional alarm apps, ALARAL focuses on helping users build better daily routines through thoughtful design, reliable functionality, and meaningful insights.

## ✨ Features

### ⏰ Smart Alarm

* Unlimited alarms
* Recurring and one-time alarms
* Alarm labels
* Gradually increasing volume
* Custom ringtones
* Vibration support
* Alarm history
* Restore previous alarms with one tap
* Active alarm overview
* Quick enable/disable toggles

### 📅 Calendar & Reminders

* Minimal monthly calendar
* Emoji-based reminder visualization
* Silent and sound reminders
* Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly reminders
* Recurring events
* Birthday and anniversary reminders
* Reminder completion history
* Persistent reminders that never disappear automatically

### 🌙 Sleep Tracking

* Automatic sleep estimation
* Manual sleep mode
* Daily sleep duration
* Weekly and monthly insights
* Sleep consistency tracking
* Average bedtime and wake-up time
* Beautiful sleep heatmap
* Sleep statistics dashboard

### 💧 Water Tracker

* Animated water bottle
* Daily hydration goal
* Quick-add water buttons
* Custom water amounts
* Smart hydration reminders
* Weekly and monthly progress
* Hydration streaks

### 📊 Dashboard

* Current time
* Next active alarm
* Today's reminders
* Sleep summary
* Water intake
* Recent alarm history
* Personal productivity statistics

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

ALARAL follows a clean and distraction-free design language inspired by modern premium software.

* Minimal Interface
* Professional Black & White Theme
* Smooth Animations
* Large Typography
* Clean Layouts
* Offline First
* Fast and Battery Efficient

Every screen is designed to reduce clutter while making important information immediately accessible.

## 📱 Offline First

ALARAL stores your alarms, reminders, sleep records, and water logs locally on your device. Your data remains available even without an internet connection, providing a fast, private, and reliable experience.
